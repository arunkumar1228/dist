What is Marketing cloud

Salesforce Marketing Cloud (formerly ExactTarget) is a Salesforce product that is not built on the core Salesforce platform; instead, connectors are used to sync data between your core Salesforce org and your SFMC account. Once you tap into SFMC, you will realize how powerful this feature-rich product can be.

Salesforce Marketing Cloud is a digital marketing platform that automates marketing across email, social media, mobile apps, SMS, websites, and more.

Organizations invest in Salesforce Marketing Cloud (SFMC) to combine all marketing channels in one place. Ultimately, marketers are able to send personalized messages, through the right channel, at the right time.

Features

Features of Marketing cloud​

Email Studio​

Build emails (Content Builder), preview, test, and send, also tracking campaign member emails​.

Mobile Studio​

Create, send, and monitor SMS (MobileConnect, GroupConnect). Send push notifications (plus silent notifications, in-app messaging, Bluetooth Beacons, and Geo-location (MobilePush)).​

Automation Studio​​

Create marketing automation and data management activities, then add them to the Journey Builder canvas to make multi-step automated workflows. Example activities include import file, file transfer, data extract, SQL query, filter, and script.​

Content Builder​

Build emails and landing page templates – including reusable/interactive content blocks.​

Journey Builder

An amazing campaign automation building experience that visually maps out the ‘journey’ contacts will make through your marketing touchpoints. Segment contacts into further targetted audiences using paths, and leverage a sidebar of different entry sources, activities (eg. send SMS) and flows.​
