The business value of  Customer 360

Salesforce Customer 360 architecture connects your IT, service, marketing, sales, and commerce operations to provide a single complete view of every customer process across the cycle.

Every out-of-the-box functionality comes pre-defined in the Salesforce Customer 360 tool. Service Cloud is pre-established in the Salesforce 360 connection. Email and drip campaigns in Marketing Cloud leverage content and data from Customer 360 platform. All data and events in Commerce Cloud are sent to Customer 360 connections.​

Solutions

Challenges resolved by Customer 360

Siloed Data​

Integrating data from various sources, such as sales, service, marketing, and commerce, into a single unified platform. This allows businesses to have a holistic view of their customers and access all relevant information in one place​.

Incomplete Customer Profile​

Customer 360 addresses this challenge by collecting data from multiple channels and touchpoints, including interactions from email, social media, website visits, and customer support.​

Inefficient data analysis​

Customer 360 leverages Salesforce's AI capabilities, such as Einstein Analytics, to process and analyze vast amounts of customer data. It provides businesses with actionable insights, predictive analytics.

Lack of Collaboration​

Collaboration across teams is crucial for effective CRM. Salesforce Customer 360 provides a centralized platform where teams can collaborate, share customer information.​

Scalability and flexibility​

CRM systems need to be scalable and adaptable to meet evolving business needs. Salesforce Customer 360 offers a cloud-based platform that can scale with growing data volumes and user demands.​
