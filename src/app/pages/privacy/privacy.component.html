Cookies and other Tracking Technologies

QBrainX employs cookies to enhance the ease and significance of interactions on the Company's websites. When you visit any of the Company's websites, QBrainX (or one of our third-party service providers') servers transmits a cookie to your computer. Cookies are pieces of information that a website transfers to your hard drive, serving to store and, at times, track information about you. While most web browsers automatically accept cookies, you have the option to modify your browser settings to prevent this. Instructions on how to do so can be found in your browser's help screen or manual. It's important to note that choosing to disable cookies may limit your ability to fully leverage our websites, software, or services.

QBrainX utilizes both session-based and persistent-based cookies. Session cookies are active only during a single session, disappearing from your computer when you close your browser software or turn off your computer. In contrast, persistent cookies remain on your computer even after you close your browser or turn off your computer. This distinction allows us to tailor and enhance your experience on our websites based on your preferences and interactions.

Cookies play a vital role in enabling the Company to recognize and tally the number of visitors, as well as observe how visitors navigate our websites during their usage. This serves our legitimate purposes by facilitating improvements to the functionality of our websites. For instance, cookies assist in ensuring that users easily find what they are seeking. Additionally, cookies are employed to recognize you upon your return to our websites, contributing to a personalized and streamlined user experience.
